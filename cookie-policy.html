Cookie Policy

Detailed information on how we use cookies and tracking technologies.

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, smartphone, or other devices when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function efficiently, improve user experience, and provide analytical information to the site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

RoboYuwa categorizes the cookies we use as follows:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to operate. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas or fill out forms. They cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance and Analytical Cookies: These allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and see how visitors move around our website. This helps us improve the way our website works, for instance, by ensuring that users find what they are looking for easily.
  • Functionality Cookies: These are used to recognize you when you return to our website. This enables us to personalize our content for you and remember your preferences (e.g., language or region).

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies (such as Google Analytics) to report usage statistics and deliver embedded features (like video players or social media feeds). These third parties may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by modifying your browser settings. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may impact the functionality and features of the RoboYuwa website.

To learn more about how to manage cookies on popular browsers, please visit their respective support pages (e.g., Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari).

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

RoboYuwa may periodically update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this page regularly for the latest information on our use of cookies.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or queries regarding this Cookie Policy, please contact our Legalities Department at roboyuwa@gmail.com.
