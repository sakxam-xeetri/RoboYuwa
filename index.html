Who We Are

Founded in June 2024, RoboYuwa is a youth-driven NGO focused on robotics, technology, research, and innovation. We believe in providing a dedicated technical platform for youth to explore their potential.

Our core purpose is to build an innovative mindset among the youth and develop their leadership, confidence, and creative problem-solving skills in the field of robotics and technical research. Through collaboration and hands-on learning, we unite young minds for science and technology.

  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Equality & Inclusiveness
  • Ethical Technology Use & Community Impact

Our Mission

To empower youth with technology skills, creative problem-solving, and leadership to transform communities.

Our Vision

A society where ethical technology and innovation are driven by capable, confident, and united youth creating sustainable community impact.

Key Objectives

01

Promote hands-on learning, innovation, and creativity in robotics and technology.

02

Develop leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving through projects, workshops, and community programs.

03

Unite young minds for science and technology collaboration and foster an ethical approach.

04

Build robotics & technology projects and actively participate in national and international competitions.

Major Initiatives

RoboLearn Initiative

A structured 12-week robotics curriculum specifically designed for underserved schools, bringing essential tech education to communities that need it most.

InnovatYouth Summit

An annual youth solution showcase focusing on empowering young creators. Participants present innovative projects judged by industry experts.

Tech4Community

Empowering youth to design and develop low-cost technical solutions addressing the immediate needs of local organizations and the community.
