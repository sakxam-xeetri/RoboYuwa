About RoboYuwa

Empowering the next generation of tech leaders in Nepal through hands-on robotics, research, and innovation.

Our Story

Founded in June 2024, RoboYuwa is a youth-driven non-governmental organization entirely dedicated to robotics, technology, research, and innovation. We emerged from a shared vision to provide a technical platform exclusively for youth, enabling them to explore their potential without barriers.

Our journey began with a singular goal: to develop a generation of practical problem solvers. Just months after our inception, we proudly represented Nepal at the Technoxian World Robotics Championship in August 2024. Since then, we have expanded our reach to multiple districts, impacting over 1,200 young minds through 40+ structured workshops.

At RoboYuwa, we operate purely on the core values of Transparency, Accountability, Equality, Inclusiveness, and Ethical Technology Use.
