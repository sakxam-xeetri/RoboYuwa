Privacy Policy

Comprehensive details on how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RoboYuwa. The privacy and security of your personal data are of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how RoboYuwa ("we", "our", or "us"), guided by our Legalities Department, collects, processes, and protects information when you visit our website, apply for membership, or participate in our programs and events.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and mailing address.
  • Educational and Professional Data: Institution name, academic major, technical skills, and organizational affiliations.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you navigate our website, including IP addresses, browser types, and device information (automatically collected).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used for legitimate organizational purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Processing membership applications and maintaining centralized human resource records.
  • Communicating updates regarding workshops, hackathons, and RoboYuwa initiatives.
  • Managing event logistics, volunteer coordination, and interdepartmental communications.
  • Improving our digital platforms and tailoring content to user preferences.
  • Ensuring compliance with governing laws and our internal legal and administrative frameworks.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data only under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party platforms (e.g., event platforms, email providers) operating under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: If required to do so by law, or to protect the safety, rights, and property of RoboYuwa and its members.
  • Partnerships: Aggregated, non-personally identifiable data may be shared with sponsors and partners to demonstrate organizational impact.

5. Data Security

In accordance with our Data Privacy & Confidentiality Policy, we enforce strict procedural and technical safeguards. We utilize secure servers, restricted access protocols, and encryption to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, or as required by applicable laws and our internal documentation frameworks.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to request corrections to any inaccuracies, and to request the deletion of your data (subject to certain legal limitations). To exercise these rights, please contact our Legalities Department.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: roboyuwa@gmail.com
